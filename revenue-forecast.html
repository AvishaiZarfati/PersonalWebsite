Next‑Quarter Revenue Forecast

Gradient Boosting model (PyCaret + XGBoost) predicting B2B quarterly revenue by integrating product‑feature metrics, market sentiment indices and competitor launches. Achieved MAPE 5.7 % on back‑test.

Workflow Highlights

  • Aggregated historical revenue + 12 external drivers into a feature matrix with BigQuery SQL.
  • Used PyCaret automated modelling to benchmark 15 algorithms; LightGBM & XGB were top performers.
  • Hyper‑tuned via Optuna; retraining pipeline scheduled weekly with Cloud Functions.
  • Visualised forecasts & confidence intervals in Tableau for stakeholder dashboards.

Business Results

  • Enabled finance team to lock marketing budgets 6 weeks earlier.
  • Identified market‑sentiment sensitivity, guiding roadmap prioritisation.
