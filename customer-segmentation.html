Customer Segmentation Engine

A scalable clustering pipeline built in Google BigQuery ML that segmented 1 million+ shoppers into actionable cohorts, enabling personalised marketing campaigns and boosting ROAS by 25 %.

Key Responsibilities

  • Cleaned and denormalised 18 months of transaction & site‑behaviour data (900 GB) with SQL.
  • Engineered RFM‑style and behavioural features; reduced dimensionality via PCA.
  • Trained & evaluated kmeans models in BigQuery ML, selecting k = 7 via silhouette score.
  • Deployed scheduled clustering to BigQuery using Cloud Composer; dashboards auto‑refresh in Looker Studio.

Impact

  • Campaign ROAS ↑ 25 % within 2 months by targeting high‑value clusters.
  • Churn cluster predictive model fed retention emails, reducing churn by 8 % QoQ.
